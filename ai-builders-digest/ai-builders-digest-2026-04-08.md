Paper Review is built to act more like a careful technical reviewer than a grammar checker. It looks for issues in math, derivations, notation, units, structure, and whether a paper's claims are actually supported by the results. It also catches inconsistencies across sections, figures, tables, captions, and appendices. The goal is to help authors strengthen technical rigor and correctness before submission.

Paper Review 被设计成一个仔细的技术审稿人，而不是语法检查器。它检查数学、推导、符号、单位、结构方面的问题，以及论文的结论是否被结果真正支撑。它还能发现各章节、图表、表格、图注和附录之间的不一致。目标是帮助作者在提交前加强技术严谨性和正确性。

Inside Prism, it runs as a structured workflow and writes its output as an editable LaTeX review file directly in the project. That means the review becomes part of the paper workspace itself, so authors can revise and work through feedback in the same environment as the manuscript.

在 Prism 中，它以结构化工作流的方式运行，并将输出直接写入项目中的可编辑 LaTeX 审稿文件。这意味着审稿意见成为论文工作区本身的一部分，作者可以在与手稿相同的环境中修改和处理反馈。

它还会在提交前标记表述不清、引用问题和校对错误。
